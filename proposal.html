Project Proposal | WP

Description:

VS is a vehicle sharing marketplace where guests can book any vehicle they want, wherever they want it, from a vibrant community of local hosts across Pakistan. Guests choose from a totally unique selection of nearby vehicles, while hosts earn extra money to offset the costs of vehicle ownership.
Unlike traditional car-rental services, VS does not own or maintain any vehicle. Instead it offers a platform on which car owners can rent their cars. People who wish to rent their vehicle can register their cars online to be rented by others. The owner states when and where the vehicle will be available. A customer who wants to rent a car reserves a specific time slot for the car online. VS will take 5 to 20 percent of rental income, depending on the insurance coverage it provides the vehicle owner.
As a matter of fact, the average car is underutilized 65% of the time so we intend to:
• Maximize the value of a depreciating asset
• Make renting a vehicle easier for people
• Make renting a vehicle affordable
• Make renting a vehicle an unique and fun experience
